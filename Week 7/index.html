Kittens

by Petronela Lazar

Newborn kittens are completely helpless and totally dependent on their mothers for nourishment, warmth and elimination. If they're orphaned, they need 24/7 care from a kind human. They need to stay warm and eat every couple of hours around the clock. They even need help pottying. From birth to about 5 weeks old, kittens rely on their mothers to stimulate their anogenital region so they can urinate and defecate.
